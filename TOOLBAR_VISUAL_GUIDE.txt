WORKFLOW EXAMPLE:

1. Draw a rectangle:
   - Press W (wall tool)
   - Click top-left corner → Click top-right corner
   - Click top-right corner → Click bottom-right corner
   - Click bottom-right corner → Click bottom-left corner
   - Click bottom-left corner → Click top-left corner to close

2. Assign room type:
   - Press R (room tool)
   - Click inside the rectangle
   - Select room type from popup (Bedroom, Kitchen, etc.)

3. Add door:
   - Press D (door tool)
   - Click on a wall to place door
   - Drag if you want to reposition it

4. Move a wall:
   - Click the wall to select it
   - Grab the gold circle in the middle
   - Drag to new position
   - Release to place

5. Delete a wall:
   - Press X (delete tool)
   - Click the wall you want to remove
   - Wall is instantly deleted

6. Undo a mistake:
   - Press Ctrl+Z to undo
   - Press Ctrl+Y to redo
